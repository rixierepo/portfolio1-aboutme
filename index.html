About me

My name is Richo Malvin, a freelancer in hardware engineering and programming especially in microcontroller devices that use the arduino programming language such as Arduino boards, Espressif, stm32, and similar products. I have been interested in electronics since childhood but only as a hobby. I have a background in computer and network engineering when I entered vocational high school, and that's where I got this expertise. When I entered college, my interest in hardware engineering reboosted maybe because I took part in robotics student activities, and that's where for the first time I know what is Arduino, how Arduino works, how to program Arduino, how to make automation devices, robots, etc. I think my skills and background maybe can be combined, hardware and computer network. Now these two technologies are combined with another technologies and born a new revolutionary things called Internet of Things.
